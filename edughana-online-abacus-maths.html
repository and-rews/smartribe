Abacus Mathematics

An old age tool used for calculation purposes with many benefits!

[ Your Child's Brain Would Be Developed ]

At SmartTribe, we understand that parents and students have busy schedules and may not be able to attend onsite training sessions for abacus. To ensure that every child has access to quality abacus training, we offer online abacus training.

Our online abacus training is designed to provide the same level of instruction and support as our onsite training. We use the same curriculum, materials, and teaching methods to ensure that students receive a comprehensive and effective learning experience.
